UvaUve Foods – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

UvaUve Foods LLP (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the UvaUve Foods mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information

  • Device model, OS version
  • Crash logs & performance data
  • App usage analytics

1.2 Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) Token

We collect your device's FCM token to send push notifications such as order updates, offers, and announcements.

1.3 Optional Information

If you interact with our website or contact us, we may receive:

  • Name
  • Phone number

No payments or orders are made inside the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send push notifications
  • To improve app experience and performance
  • To debug crashes and app issues

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices and trusted services like Firebase. Although no method is fully secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications at any time from device settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to stop all data collection.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
